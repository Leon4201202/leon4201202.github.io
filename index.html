About the Company

A Different
Kind of Foundation

Orion Tech LLC is an independent AI infrastructure company. We don't build apps on top of existing AI platforms. We build the underlying architecture — the coordination systems, the memory frameworks, the structural foundations that make large-scale AI operation possible.

Our approach comes from an unexpected place: over a decade and a half of experience in high-precision manufacturing systems, where distributed coordination isn't a concept — it's a daily operational requirement. We brought that thinking into AI infrastructure and built from first principles.

"There is something being built here that the industry hasn't seen yet. We're not ready to show all of it. But the foundation is real, it's working, and it's further along than most people would expect."

We are early-stage by choice. We move carefully because what we're building demands it. When the time is right, the full picture will speak for itself.
